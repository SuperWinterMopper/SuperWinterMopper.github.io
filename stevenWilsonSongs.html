STEVEN WILSON SONGS

My favorite Steven Wilson songs, ranked from #100 to #1. Why? Because Wilson is my favorite musician, and if I were to have a personal hero, it would be him. I have an intimate relationship with much of his music. To me, his career is a true work of art. This list covers many of Wilson's projects, including Porcupine Tree, No-Man, Storm Corrosion, and of course his solo work. Disclaimer: This list covers songs Steven wrote or co-wrote. It doesn't cover the songs he's remixed, produced or featured on as a guest.
