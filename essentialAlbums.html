100 ESSENTIAL

ALBUMS

The 100 albums closest to my heart, in release date order.

I'd be happy if you looked at this with a lighthearted lens. These aren't the 100 most influential albums that I've happened to listen to. They're simply the ones that are profound in my musical journey, and for that I think my musical DNA is pretty clear here.

If you think my list is awful, that's pretty cool! It's our differences that make it interesting.

You might find that there are certain musical approaches that I'm very drawn to. (hint: 1973 is my favorite year in music)

Enjoy!
