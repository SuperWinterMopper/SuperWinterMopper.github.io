100 ESSENTIAL

ALBUMS

My 100 essential albums, in release date order.

I'd be happy if you looked at this with a lighthearted lens. These aren't the 100 most cricitally acclaimed, innovative albums I've listened to. They're simply the ones that are essential to my musical journey, and for that I think my musical DNA is pretty clear here.

I of course love all of these albums, but if you think my list is awful, that's fine too! It's our differences that make it interesting.

You're quickly going to find that there are certain musical approaches that I'm VERY drawn to. (hint: 1973 is my favorite year in music)

Enjoy!

#1 Days of Future Passed

The Moody Blues

10 November 1967

It only feels right that my first album is arguably the very first progressive rock album. Honestly, this is the only Moody Blues album I would say I love, even though they have some really interesting material in later albums. Very conceptual, as with most of their work.
