100 ESSENTIAL

ALBUMS part19

My 100 essential albums, in release date order.

I'd be happy if you looked at this with a lighthearted lens. These aren't the 100 most cricitally acclaimed, innovative albums I've listened to. They're simply the ones that are essential to my musical journey, and for that I think my musical DNA is pretty clear here. Also, keep in mind I was 17 when this list was made.

I of course love all of these albums, but if you think my list is awful, that's fine too! It's our differences that make it interesting.

You might find that there are certain musical approaches that I'm very drawn to. (hint: 1973 is my favorite year in music)

Enjoy!
